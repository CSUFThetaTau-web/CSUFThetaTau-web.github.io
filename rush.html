Level Up  with Theta Tau




Theta Tau is a co-ed engineering fraternity at CSUF. The foundation formed here at Theta Tau has culitvated a community that strives to uphold its three pillars of Professionalism, Community Service and Brotherhood. With new members joining every semester, creativity is exploited and new avenues are explored. This is an organization built on building careers, but also building the individual. That doesn't go without saying that Theta Tau is a family where your brother's bond is unbreakable.
